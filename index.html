About Me

Greetings and welcome to my homepage! I'm a librarian at Case Western Reserve University and I'm looking forward to delving into the wild and woolly world of web development.

My husband and I live in the Cleveland area with our rambunctious cat, Clemmie. In my free time, I enjoy Zumba, yoga, reading and exploring the delights that Cleveland has to offer.

People are often confused how to say my last name. The first syllable is pronounced “cur” like a mean dog. The second and last syllables are pronounced like the last name Kessler.
